# Newborn Care: The First 28 Days

The neonatal period is highly sensitive and requires timely screening, feeding support, and danger sign detection.

## Why This Matters

This infographic belongs to the theme *Maternal and Child Health* and section *Newborn Care Essentials*. It provides concise guidance on early neonatal assessment and stabilization.

## Key Focus Points

[[[
- *Immediate postnatal assessment*
- *Thermal and feeding support*
- *Neonatal jaundice monitoring*
- *Parent education*
]]]


## Red Flags

[[[
- *Escalate when:* poor feeding, lethargy, cyanosis, fever/hypothermia, or apnea
- *Avoid:* delaying referral when danger signs persist
]]]
