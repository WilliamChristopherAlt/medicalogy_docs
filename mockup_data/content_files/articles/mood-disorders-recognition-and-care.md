## Decision Matrix

| Situation | Immediate Action | Escalation Trigger |
|-----------|------------------|--------------------|
| Stable but symptomatic | Evaluate mood symptoms, sleep, appetite, and suicide risk | No improvement after first cycle |
| Progressive deterioration | Activate rapid team support | Persistent red flags after reassessment |
| High-risk baseline | Parallel treatment and monitoring | Active suicidality, psychosis, severe functional decline, or mania |
