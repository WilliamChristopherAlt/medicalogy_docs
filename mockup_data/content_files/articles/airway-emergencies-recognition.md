# Airway Emergencies: Recognition and First Actions

Airway compromise can become fatal within minutes. Rapid recognition and immediate intervention are essential. This page is a full-fledged reference article with practical pathways, risk markers, and implementation guidance.

## Decision Matrix

| Situation | Immediate Action | Escalation Trigger |
|-----------|------------------|--------------------|
| Stable but symptomatic | Open airway, call support, and monitor breathing continuously | No improvement after first cycle |
| Progressive deterioration | Activate rapid team support | Persistent red flags after reassessment |
| High-risk baseline | Parallel treatment and monitoring | Stridor, cyanosis, decreasing consciousness, or no airflow |

[[[
